# Global Layout Components

## Navbar
- **Sticky Positioning:** Remains at the top of the viewport during scroll.
- **Zustand Sync:** Controls the opening/closing of the WCAG Accessibility menu.
- **Responsive:** Collapses into a mobile-friendly menu for smaller viewports.

## Footer
- **Navigation Links:** Provides direct access to support, FAQs, and government links.
- **Social Integration:** Placeholders for social media presence.
- **Brand Identity:** Reinforces the formal identification of the Malaysian Judiciary system.
- **Copyright:** Dynamic year display.
